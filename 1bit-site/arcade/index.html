Why it lives here and not on /demo/

The /demo/ pages are static HTML with attribute-driven counters — a marketing surface, not an interactive toy. /arcade/ is where the ported WASM kernels themselves run. Separate URL, separate caching strategy, separate contract with the visitor: arcade pages will actually use their CPU.
