Three steps. About 90 seconds. Your phone talks to your halo box over the private mesh — not to a cloud.

  1. add 1bit.voice to your home screen

    Makes it feel like an app. No app store, no review, no install-prompt nags.

    iOS · SafariShare → Add to Home Screen
    Android · Chrome⋮ menu → Install app

  2. join your mesh

    Install Tailscale (or Headscale client) on the phone, then authenticate with the key bong DM's you. Your phone gets 100.64.0.*.

  3. open 1bit.voice and tap to start

    Allow mic access. Press and hold the cyan orb. Release to send. First audio comes back in about 1.2 seconds.
