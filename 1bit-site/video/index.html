1bit.video · part of 1bit.systems

Video catalogs in megabytes.

Same architecture as 1bit.music, applied to Wan 2.2 TI2V-5B video tokens. Free tier reconstructs via a tiny ternary diffusion transformer. Premium is arithmetic-coded residual for bit-identical playback of the original source.

Gated on training

Ternary DiT retrain is Run 7 in our queue (launch sequence). Blocked on our 1bit-ggml 5D tensor loader patch. ETA measured in weeks, not months. Subscribe to the research feed for status.

Pipeline

source video → Wan 2.2 TI2V encoder (tokenised) → ternary LM memorises token stream (free tier: ~5 MB / clip) → arithmetic coder packs bit-perfect residual (Premium: ~30% below raw) → .1bl video container → decode on client gfx1151 / iGPU via our HIP ternary kernel
