What qualifies as news

  • Public release (v1.0, AppImage / Flatpak / PKGBUILD shipping).

  • Milestone completions (NPU unblock, Sparse-BitNet retrain shipping, Medusa integration).

What does not qualify

  • Internal milestones — those live in the docs changelog.
  • Blog posts — those live on the blog.
  • Social commentary.
