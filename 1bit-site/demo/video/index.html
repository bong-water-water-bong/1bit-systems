1984

1949 novel by George Orwell · film adaptations 1956 (Anderson), 1984 (Radford)

Orwell died 1950. Life+50 reached on January 1, 2001 under the pre-2022 Canadian term — his works entered Canadian PD then and are grandfathered through the life+70 extension. The 1956 and 1984 screen adaptations have their own cinematographic terms; 1956 cleared Canadian PD in 2006, 1984 in 2034. We'll ingest the novel as an audiobook (via our TTS engine post Run 6) and the film adaptations as they become unambiguously clear.

Triage queue

Each title gets a per-film copyright audit (composition, performance, and score rights can outlive the cinematographic work's term — verifying Canadian-publication date and checking CISAC/SOCAN on the music). We ingest once rights are confirmed clean. For works whose components have tangled rights (US-commissioned scores, Broadway soundtracks, etc.) we hold until each component clears.

Same architecture, different kernel

The lossy free tier uses a ternary DiT (10–40 M parameters depending on video resolution) trained to memorise the film's Wan 2.2 token stream. The lossless Premium tier is the same DiT as the probability model inside an arithmetic coder over the original MP4 bytestream — identical sha256 back to the source.
