What you'll hear here after training

  • Free tier comparison: the same track reconstructed from the 10 M-parameter ternary LM + Mimi codec. ~2 MB model ships once, this specific track is decoded from those weights on-device.
  • Premium comparison: the same track losslessly reproduced from model + arithmetic-coded residual. Byte-identical to the original, checksummable.
