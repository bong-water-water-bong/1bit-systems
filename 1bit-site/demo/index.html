How the meter works

The HUD up top uses the browser's Performance API to sum transferred bytes for every audio resource played this session. Compare that against the sum of 1bit.music per-track projections (shown in the saved-column). The surplus is measured, not claimed.

Caveat: the projection is amortised. The 2 MB of ternary LM weights ship once per catalog; the per-track figures divide that cost across the catalog's track count. A listener who plays one track and stops downloads more than 2 MB; a listener who plays the whole catalog downloads ~100 KB per track effective.
